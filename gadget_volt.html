Founded after the Brexit vote, Volt is a pan-european, participative and progressive movement. We are active in 32 countries and have registered political parties in 13 with our goal of trying to be the voice for all those that believe in the necessity of a stronger, more democratic and transparent Europe.

We want to use this calendar to present our program for the upcoming European elections, called the Amsterdam Declaration. The Declaration was created by Volt volunteers from all over Europe and accepted on our national assembly in Amsterdam. We will introduce one of our propositions every day until the end of the year.

Follow the calendar, share and discuss proposals and concrete steps with us on social media or join us to help adapt policies on national and local levels as well as to prepare for the European Elections
